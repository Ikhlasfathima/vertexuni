Our Courses

Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)

This undergraduate program focuses on computer science, programming languages, and software development. Students learn to build applications, manage databases, and understand IT systems.

  • Duration: 3 Years
  • Eligibility: High School Graduate (10+2)
  • Career Opportunities: Software Developer, System Analyst, IT Consultant

Master of Computer Applications (MCA)

The MCA program offers in-depth knowledge of computer science and programming. The course covers areas like artificial intelligence, machine learning, cloud computing, and database management.

  • Duration: 2 Years
  • Eligibility: Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science or related field
  • Career Opportunities: Software Engineer, Database Administrator, Systems Architect

B.Sc. in Computer Science

This undergraduate program provides knowledge of basic and advanced concepts in computer science. It focuses on programming, data structures, algorithms, and software engineering.

  • Duration: 3 Years
  • Eligibility: High School Graduate (10+2)
  • Career Opportunities: Software Developer, Network Engineer, IT Support Specialist

B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering

B.Tech in CSE offers advanced study in computing, programming, software engineering, and hardware design. It is ideal for students seeking careers in software development or IT infrastructure.

  • Duration: 4 Years
  • Eligibility: High School Graduate (10+2) with Physics, Chemistry, and Math
  • Career Opportunities: Software Engineer, Network Architect, Systems Engineer

Diploma in Data Science & AI

This diploma program focuses on data analysis, artificial intelligence, machine learning, and big data technologies. It prepares students for roles in data science and analytics.

  • Duration: 1 Year
  • Eligibility: Bachelor's Degree or Equivalent
  • Career Opportunities: Data Scientist, Machine Learning Engineer, Data Analyst

Certificate in Cybersecurity

This certificate program covers essential cybersecurity concepts, ethical hacking, network security, and risk management. It is designed for those looking to specialize in cybersecurity.

  • Duration: 6 Months
  • Eligibility: Bachelor's Degree or Equivalent
  • Career Opportunities: Cybersecurity Analyst, Ethical Hacker, Security Consultant
